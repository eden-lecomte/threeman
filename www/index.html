How to play

Firstly, don't be a dick

General

Roll the dice until no one drinks as a result of your roll. If you roll nothing on the first roll of your turn, this is considered a 'virgin roll' - drink and roll again.

Three Man

When a are rolled, this person becomes the Three Man. They must drink to introduce themselves, and put on a silly hat.

  • Any time a appears on either dice, the Three Man drinks
  • , mean the Three Man must drink 3 - "3 for 3 man"
  • If the current Three Man rolls a , then he/she gets to nominate a new Three Man
  • Any time a new Three Man is introduced, the current and new Three Man must drink - "Salutations"
  • Under no circumstances is the Three Man allowed to stop playing while they are the current Three Man

Social - 10

Any time a total of 10 is rolled, everyone playing must drink.

Duels

Two players must duel by each rolling a dice, with the loser drinking the difference. The roll is also counted as a roll from the person who set up the duel. You could become Three Man as a result of the duel!

Additional rules

There are some additional courtesy rules to play by.

  • Do not touch the phone unless it is your turn, or take a drink (the owner of the phone is exempt from this rule)
  • If you accept the phone from someone's hands, then you must finish your drink

Off the table

In the tabletop version of Three Man, if you roll the dice off the table you must either finish your drink or become the new Three Man (or both).
